Recent Publications

Our latest contributions to the AI research community

NEURIPS 2025

Post-Thinking: A Novel Architecture for Multi-Layer Cognitive Processing

Chen, L., Rodriguez, M., Patel, A., Zhang, W. — Protoetic Research

We introduce post-thinking architecture, a paradigm shift in neural network design that enables multiple reasoning processes to collaborate and self-correct in real-time. Our approach achieves state-of-the-art performance on complex reasoning benchmarks while maintaining full interpretability of intermediate steps.

PDF Code arXiv
ICML 2025

Extended Reasoning Chains: Scaling Cognitive Depth in Language Models

Patel, A., Kim, J., Thompson, R. — Protoetic Research

This paper presents a framework for training language models to engage in extended reasoning chains, enabling them to solve complex multi-step problems that require planning, hypothesis testing, and iterative refinement. We demonstrate significant improvements on mathematical reasoning and code generation tasks.

PDF Code arXiv
AAAI 2025

Transparent AI: Ensuring Interpretability in Deep Cognitive Systems

Zhang, W., Anderson, K., Chen, L. — Protoetic Research

We propose a comprehensive framework for building interpretable AI systems that can explain their reasoning processes at every step. Our approach combines attention visualization, intermediate representation analysis, and natural language explanations to provide full transparency without sacrificing performance.

PDF arXiv
ICLR 2025

Safety-First Neural Architectures: Design Principles for Aligned AI

Rodriguez, M., Liu, S., Anderson, K. — Protoetic Research

This work establishes design principles for building AI systems that are inherently safer and more aligned with human values. We introduce architectural constraints that prevent harmful outputs while maintaining model capability and demonstrate their effectiveness across multiple domains.
